How to Get the Most Reach

  1. Submit to the CVB first. Their online form is the fastest. This gets you in front of tourists and locals who use Visit Huntington as their go-to.
  2. Email the Herald-Dispatch. Include date, time, location, a short description, and a contact number. They'll add it to their print and digital calendar.
  3. List on Eventbrite. Even for free events. It ranks well on Google and gives people a way to RSVP so you can gauge interest.
  4. Post to Patch & Facebook. Patch is free and reaches local readers. A Facebook Event in Huntington community groups rounds out your digital footprint.
  5. Contact the venue. If your event is at Pullman Square, Old Central City, or another established venue, ask them to cross-list it on their calendar too.
